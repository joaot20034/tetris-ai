# Glossary

## Agent

The AI making decisions.

---

## Environment

The world the agent interacts with.

---

## State

The current observation.

---

## Action

A decision made by the agent.

---

## Reward

Feedback received after an action.

---

## Episode

One complete game.

---

## Policy

The strategy used by the agent.

---

## Q-Value

Predicted future reward of taking an action.

---

## Replay Buffer

Memory storing previous experiences.

---

## Target Network

Frozen neural network used for stable learning.

---

## Exploration

Trying random actions.

---

## Exploitation

Using the best known action.

---

## Bellman Equation

The recursive equation underlying Q-learning.

---

## Gamma

Discount factor.

---

## Neural Network

Function approximator replacing the Q-Table.

---

## Backpropagation

Algorithm that updates network weights.

---

## Gradient Descent

Optimization algorithm minimizing prediction error.

---

## CNN

Convolutional Neural Network.

Used to extract spatial features from the Tetris board.
